Where's Tuffy?

"Where is Tuffy?” is a digital game that adds the player somewhere in the Cal State University, Fullerton panorama map and allows them to guess their location based on clues. The user will be timed on how fast they can guess their location, and if they can guess before their opponent. This will be a completely free game that allows users to create a profile that will save all the information of their past games. A google authenticator service will make sure that applications are secured using this multi- factored authentication mechanism. Some key features in the game include a leaderboard, where the players will witness the users in the top spot and the fastest completion time. A tutorial that allows new players to go through a step-by-step gameplay to better understand the way to play the game. Having a single player mode allows users to enjoy playing games without any pressure of competition. This game is set to charm a wide audience that includes new students, casual gamers, educators, and anyone who has trouble navigating the layout of the school. In conclusion, by having its leaderboards, tutorials, and single-player mode, “Where is Tuffy?” will captivate a huge audience and have students exploring areas of the school that are underrated.
